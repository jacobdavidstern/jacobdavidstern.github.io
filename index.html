About Me

Hi! I'm Jacob, and I began my journey studying computer science at Washington State University, where I built a strong foundation in problem‑solving and technical thinking. Though I shifted paths at the time, that early exposure to software development planted the seed for my current pursuit of full‑stack engineering.

My professional background includes several years in technical support, where I became the go‑to resource for complex issues. At Hometown Ticketing, I trained peers on mobile platforms and resolved a critical system flaw that generated over 17,000 support requests in a single week. These experiences sharpened my ability to diagnose problems, communicate solutions, and collaborate across teams.

Today, I’m channeling that same persistence and curiosity into full‑stack development. Through my bootcamp training, I’m building responsive, semantic, and scalable applications while applying the same stewardship mindset that guided my support career. My goal is to create software that is not only functional, but intuitive, reliable, and empowering for users.
